Kickin' Collard Greens

Description

Well, hey, sug! Welcome to a little guide on how to create some Southern-styled Kickin' Collard Greens!

Ingredients

  • One tablespoon olive oil
  • Three slices bacon
  • One large onion, chopped
  • Two cloves garlic, minced
  • One pound fresh collard greens, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • Three cups chicken broth
  • One teaspoon salt
  • One teaspoon pepper
  • One pinch red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Gather all of your ingredients.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add bacon and cook until crisp for 5 to 7 minutes. Remove bacon from pan, crumble and then return to the pan.
  3. Add onion to the pan and cook until tender for about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook until just fragrant. Add collard greens and fry until they begin to wilt.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and season salt, pepper and red pepper flakes. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until the greens are tender (roughly 45 minutes).
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
