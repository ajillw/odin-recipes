Mom's Applesauce Pancakes

Description

Hello and welcome! This is how to make a blast from the past, Mom's Applesauce Pancakes!

Ingredients

  • Two cups dry pancake mix
  • One teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Two large eggs
  • One cup applesauce
  • One teaspoon lemon juice
  • Half cup milk

Steps

  1. Stir pancake mix and cinnamon together in a large bowl; make a well in the center.
  2. Add eggs, applesauce, lemon juice and milk. Stir mixture until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoup the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and the edge are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
  4. Enjoy!
