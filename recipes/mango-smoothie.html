A Simple Mango Smoothie

Description

Here is how you make a very simple and smooth mango smoothie!

Ingredients

  • Two mangos - peeled, seeded, and sliced
  • Two bananas
  • Two cups vanilla yogurt
  • Two cups milk

Steps

  1. Add mangos, banana (sliced in half), vanilla yogurt, and milk into a large blender.
  2. Blend the ingredients until smooth. If the smoothie is too thick, proceed to add more milk as needed for a smoother consistency.
  3. Serve immediately.
